# Prompt Library — Solo Escena (Persona sin Producto)

Ejemplos de prompts para templates donde Gemini agrega una **persona a la escena SIN mostrar el producto**.
La persona siempre va en el lado opuesto al copy. El producto NO debe aparecer en el frame.

Tipos de ángulo: `pain-point` | `aspiracional` | `lifestyle` | `testimonial`

---

## Pain Point / Problema

Usar cuando el copy habla del DOLOR que el producto resuelve. La persona refleja ese dolor específico.

**Skincare — problema de piel (manchas, arrugas, acné):**
Woman in her early 30s, natural minimal makeup, wearing a simple white t-shirt, sitting in soft bathroom lighting. She looks at her reflection with a concerned expression, gently touching her cheek or under-eye area with her fingertips. Shoulders slightly hunched forward, quiet frustration. Warm ambient bathroom light. Person on right side of canvas only. DO NOT modify the left side — it contains pre-rendered copy text.

**Salud — fatiga y energía baja:**
Woman in her late 30s, tired eyes, wearing casual home clothes, sitting at a kitchen table with a half-empty coffee mug. She looks down with an exhausted expression, one hand resting on her forehead. Soft natural morning light from a window. Candid authentic moment. Person on right side of canvas only. DO NOT modify the left side — it contains pre-rendered copy text.

**Fitness — frustración sin resultados:**
Woman in her late 20s in athletic wear, standing in a gym or at home. Looking down at herself with a frustrated or disappointed expression, arms crossed lightly, weight shifted to one side. Body language conveys stagnation despite effort. Gym or home ambient lighting. Person on right side of canvas only. DO NOT modify the left side — it contains pre-rendered copy text.

**Digestión / Inflamación / Hinchazón:**
Woman in her 30s wearing comfortable casual clothes, sitting on a couch. One hand rests on her abdomen with a pained or uncomfortable expression, body slightly leaning forward. Warm home ambient lighting, candid and relatable. Person on right side of canvas only. DO NOT modify the left side — it contains pre-rendered copy text.

**Caída del cabello / Cuidado capilar:**
Woman in her 30s–40s looking at strands of hair on her hand or brush with a worried expression. Standing in front of a mirror or in soft bathroom lighting. Vulnerability and concern in her expression. Person on right side of canvas only. DO NOT modify the left side — it contains pre-rendered copy text.

**Dolor físico / Articulaciones / Espalda:**
Woman or man in their 40s in casual clothes, sitting or standing with one hand on their lower back or neck, expression of discomfort. Not dramatic — subtle everyday pain. Warm home or office lighting. Person on right side of canvas only. DO NOT modify the left side — it contains pre-rendered copy text.
