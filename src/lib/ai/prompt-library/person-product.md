# Prompt Library — Persona con Producto

Ejemplos de prompts para templates donde la **persona SOSTIENE o USA el producto activamente**.
La persona va en el lado opuesto al copy. El producto debe ser visible, reconocible y con label hacia cámara.

---

## Belleza & Cosmética (skincare, cremas, sueros, aceites)

**Ángulo emocional — autocuidado y gratitud:**
A real-looking woman in her late 30s, not a model — slightly asymmetric face, natural brows, minimal makeup with visible skin texture: open pores on nose and cheeks, faint smile lines, subtle undereye shadow. No airbrushing. Soft worn linen blouse in ivory, fabric naturally creased at the elbows and waist, slight thread tension where it folds. She holds the product in her right hand at chest height — thumb pressed against the side, fingers curved with visible skin compression on the packaging surface, not a posed grip. Left hand applies product to her inner forearm with two fingers, skin slightly displaced under fingertip pressure, a small amount of cream visible on the skin. Grateful exhale expression — eyes softly closed or mid-blink, mouth relaxed, not performing for camera. Weight back on her heel, natural body sway. Handheld shot feel, slight camera tilt, shot on iPhone 14 Pro or 35mm film, warm afternoon window light raking across her face from the left revealing every texture. Cream background slightly out of focus. Subject and product on right side only. DO NOT modify the left side — it contains pre-rendered copy text.

**Ángulo aspiracional — orgullo del resultado:**
A real woman in her early 40s — not conventionally beautiful, distinctly human face with character: subtle nasolabial lines, natural lip asymmetry, genuine skin luminosity from hydration not retouching. Hair slightly imperfect, one side tucked behind ear, 2-3 strands across forehead. Relaxed knit top in sage or dusty rose, fabric with natural stretch marks at shoulders, ribbed texture catching the light. She holds the product with both hands at waist height, fingers interlaced under the base, thumbs resting on label — product tilted 5 degrees toward camera, not perfectly upright. Expression: the quiet pride of someone who found what actually works — closed-mouth smile, crow's feet visible, eyes warm. Natural posture, pelvis slightly back, one knee softly bent. Shot handheld 35mm, overcast window light, no fill light, shadows allowed to fall naturally on her neck and chest. Subject and product on right side only. DO NOT modify the left side — it contains pre-rendered copy text.

**Ángulo urgencia — demostración directa:**
A confident real woman in her 30s, face with character — slightly uneven skin tone, a small blemish or mark visible, natural complexion with no correction. Cotton t-shirt or fitted top in warm white or oat, slightly worn fabric, realistic neckline drape. She extends the product directly toward camera in her right hand — arm not fully extended, elbow slightly bent, fingers wrapped with knuckle tension visible, skin folds at base of fingers. Left hand flat against her cheek, fingertips pressing slightly into skin — cheek tissue subtly displaced. Direct confrontational gaze, jaw slightly forward, no smile — the look of someone who means it. Shot tight, 50mm, hard natural sidelight from left, strong shadow on right side of face. Grain visible. Expression: authentic surprise and delight, mouth slightly open, eyebrows raised naturally — not performed. Shot from slight above, handheld, soft diffused morning light, no ring light, warm window glow. Background: real home environment, slightly blurred. Subject and product on right side only. A real woman in her early 40s, face familiar and lived-in — slight asymmetry, natural undereye circles not concealed, skin with visible pores and one or two small marks that were never edited out. Hair loosely tied, a few strands fallen around her face. Faded cotton robe or oversized shirt, fabric soft and worn, natural creases at the arms. She holds the product in one hand with the casual grip of someone who's used it a hundred times — not presenting it, just reaching for it. Thumb hooked over the cap, fingers relaxed, no tension. ## Guía de adaptación por tono publicitario

| Tono | Expresión | Postura | Gesto |
|------|-----------|---------|-------|
| Emocional | Cálida, agradecida, alivio | Relajada, abierta | Toca su piel o corazón |
| Urgencia | Directa, determinada | Erguida, hacia adelante | Extiende producto a cámara |
| Aspiracional | Orgullosa, serena | Elegante, cabeza ladeada | Presenta producto con ambas manos |
| Técnico/Beneficio | Segura, informada | Neutral, profesional | Sostiene y señala |
| Social Proof | Honesta, cercana | Abierta, relajada | Sostiene a nivel pecho, sonrisa natural |
